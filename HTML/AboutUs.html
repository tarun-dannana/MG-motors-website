About Us

We have a team of over 7000 employees. This includes nearly 600 sales personnel who are backed up by a team of more than 800 technicians and after-sales professionals.

Since 1996, we have sold more than 4,00,000 new and pre-owned automobiles. We have expanded rapidly and created a strong presence in Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and Karnataka.

With 43 state-of-the-art showrooms, 44 service centers, 25 True Value outlets, and 17 Maruti Driving Schools, we have trained more than 60,000 customers, empowering them to drive confidently.

We focus on excellent customer service, stringent quality standards, and continuous training to maintain the trust that customers place in us.

Our Journey

01

The Beginning

MG Group is a multi-segment business organization with an expansive portfolio ranging from automobiles, auto financing, construction, to healthcare, hospitality and entertainment. Out spread across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and Karnataka, Varun Group is celebrated for its inspirational foundations and principles. With an annual turnover of over 7500 cr, Varun Group is one of the leading conglomerates in the country.

02

The Way It Should Be

Since 1996, our mission at MG Motors has been simple: to simplify the car buying experience for our customers. We're here to offer not just vehicles but a complete ecosystem of services, ensuring that every customer receives the highest level of satisfaction and happiness on their automotive journey.

03

Celebrating 5,00,000 Happy Customers

We're here to build relationships. The dreams of our 5,00,000+ customers define our story. We take immense pride in being the first choice for countless families for car purchase, guiding them through the joys of car ownership.

04

Expansion and Growth

MG Motors extends its services and benefits that go beyond the ordinary. Our 57 exclusive showrooms, 64 modern-day workshops, and 162 touchpoints across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and Karnataka are strategically placed to serve you better.

05

Future Goals

Your Love has earned us accolades and recognition. We are the proud recipients of the prestigious Maruti ROYAL PLATINUM award 17 times in the Balanced Scorecard, and we've claimed the title of "Champions of Champions" twice in 2013-14 and 2015-16. These honours are a testament to our relentless efforts and overall excellence, justifying our position as India's No. 1 Maruti Suzuki Dealer.
